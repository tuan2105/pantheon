Art Class - Notes & Observations

Welcome to the studio. A true artist can draw anything, from heavy ANVILS to a simple APPLE. It takes more than just A quick DART of the PEN to create a masterpiece. If your canvas gets TORN, do not RIP it further; use TAPE or some TWINE to mend it. Pay attention to the AIR and light around the TREES in the courtyard. In our still life section, you will find a GNOME, a beautiful glass MOSAIC, and a coop with live HENS. Please look at the SIGN by the door for the daily rules. Every ION of your energy should go into your work.
